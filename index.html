How I Edit

Tools I work with

I primarily edit in DaVinci Resolve for all kinds of videos — reels, music videos and brand work.

The color grading is also done in DaVinci Resolve so every shot matches in tone, contrast and mood.

I also know Adobe Premiere Pro and Photoshop for timelines, graphics and clean-ups, but DaVinci Resolve stays my main workspace.

Edit & grade flow

Reels are cut for rhythm and hook retention, keeping every beat intentional and snappy.

For narrative and music videos, the focus is story, pacing and a grade that supports the emotion of the track.

Once the master is ready, I export platform-ready versions so you can post on Instagram, YouTube and more instantly.

Where I Work

I’m available for on-ground shoots if you’re based in the Delhi NCR region.

Remote music video and reel projects are also possible if you share a clear concept — I can help build the structure, direct the shots remotely and handle the full edit and grade.
